My Strongest Self

I'm creating this page to motivate myself and others to become physically and mentally stronger. Creating a stable body starts with the mind. If we are able to stay consistent and motivated we can achieve anything. I’ve worked as an LMT for five years which has been really hard on my body. I hurt myself because I wasn’t strong enough to be doing the work that I was doing daily. I’m hoping to turn this all around and get back to feeling like the best me possible. I’m focusing on strengthening my muscles, eating clean, drinking plenty of water, and resting when I’m tired. These are the key factors towards living life as My Strongest Self. As I get stronger, my goal is to move my practice into Health Coaching, Pilates, and Ashiatsu.
